Colleen and Joshua Evans Celebrate One Year Wedding Anniversary!

June 16, 2024
blog

Youtubers Colleen and Joshua Evans celebrated their one year wedding anniversary on July 2, 2016.

On Youtube, Colleen and Joshua are known as PsychoSoprano and JoshuaDTV. Colleen is also well-known for her fictional character “Miranda Sings.” In one of Joshua’s videos, titled “Our Story” the couple says they met online through Youtube and Facebook.

Last year, the couple shared their wedding video with their fans on Youtube, which now has over 10 million views. Their story was featured on ABCNews, titled “When Youtube Stars Fall In Love.”

Both Colleen and Joshua have been busy this year with their individual projects. Joshua has been touring across the country performing for his fans. Meanwhile, Colleen has been filming her new Netflix series Haters Back Off, which is based on her fictional character Miranda Sings.

In a vlog uploaded by Colleen, she shows her fans the flowers that Joshua gave her for their anniversary. The flowers contained a note that read:

“I still choose you. Love you Colleen. -Snoopy.”

Thank you so much for being so sweet and supportive of our first year :) Just happy that she’s next to me today.
