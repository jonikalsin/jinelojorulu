Steve Young Top 5 Football Cards: A Comprehensive Look

July 12, 2024
blog

The legendary football star Steve Young made a big impression on the NFL, and in the sports card collecting world. His reach is just as deep, and collectors are dying to own one of this cards. Today, here we are to discuss the top 5 cards of Steve Young.

 

Steve Young Cards The Allure

Steve Young has been a hot commodity on the sports card collecting scene, as his rookie cards and rare variations fetch top dollar in the market, mostly in part to him being an iconic lefty Quarterback. Young might be a faded memory for some younger fans, but as one of the most-decorated quarterbacks in league history his cards are an important part of football carding lore.

1. 1997 Metal Universe Precious Metal Gems #27 Steve Young

The most valuable card of Steve Young in the current market is the 1997 Metal Universe Precious Metal Gems #27. With a limited run of 150 PSA 5 cards, this gem holds an approximate value of $1924.0, making it a prized possession for collectors.

2. 1986 Topps #374 Steve Young

Another standout card is the 1986 Topps #374 Steve Young. As one of his rookie cards, it holds a special place in the hearts of collectors and continues to be a sought-after item.

3. 1990 Score #145 Steve Young

The 1990 Score #145 Steve Young card, offers collectors an affordable yet significant piece of Young's legacy, making it a popular choice among enthusiasts.

4. 2000 Donruss Elite Passing the Torch Autograph Steve Young, Joe Montana

A testament to Young's enduring legacy, the 2000 Donruss Elite Passing the Torch insert celebrates his illustrious career alongside Joe Montana. Limited to only 50 signed copies, this card stands as a rare and valuable piece in the market.

5. 1988 Topps Steve Young #39

Rounding out the top 5 is the 1988 Topps Steve Young #39, which, despite its seemingly unimpressive appearance, serves as the first opportunity for collectors to acquire a card featuring Young as a member of the San Francisco 49ers. Its historical significance and relation to Young's journey in the NFL contribute to its appeal among collectors.
