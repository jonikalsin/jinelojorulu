Professional Life

Steven began his YouTube career on January 7, 2011, under the name ‘Sugar Pine 7.’ His channel focused on videos related to Minecraft commentaries and animated videos.

It started gaining popularity and even featured collaborations with other YouTubers like ‘AviatorGaming’, ‘SkyDoesMinecraft’, ‘MunchingBrotato’, and ‘BurtGasm’.

In 2014, Steven became one of the more popular YouTube gamers and created a second channel named ‘HWNTwo’, which was not centered on Minecraft.

Further, Steven has created animated series called “To Kill a YouTuber” and “Wild Adventures.”

Likewise, On March 16, 2015, he started his collaboration with Reina Scully on the channel under the direction of Philip DeFranco, the creator of SourceFed’.

They began a YouTube channel named ‘Super Panic Frenzy’, which featured gaming videos and other content in English and Japanese.

The channel was closed on August 9, 2016. During this time he had two very popular animated series on YouTube as well, ‘To Kill a YouTuber’ and ‘Wild Adventures.

Beyond Minecraft

By the end of 2015, he had already been disappointed with Minecraft. Similarly, he had also done almost all kinds of videos related to the game and started focusing on his original vlogs.

In 2016, he changed his channel to ‘The Steven Suptic Experience’. He further began uploading videos of fun challenges and skits.

On July 13, 2015, he posted his first video ‘Bro Threesome’ which grew to the most popular video on his rebranded channel with over 1 million views to date.

Moreover, he was cast as the main host of ‘SourceFed’ in September 2016 due to his great relationship with Philip DeFranco.

He became a member of the team until March 25, 2017, when the channel was closed. His ideas, creativity, and hosting abilities played a significant part in making ‘SourceFed’ one of the most popular channels of all time.

In 2017, he started focusing on his channel which had again been renamed ‘Sugar Pine 7’. He hired ex-‘SourceFed editor Autumn Farrell who made good changes to the quality of his videos on the channel.

Some of the most viewed videos on his channel are ‘I Hired Autumn, and Here’s Why, ‘Sourcefed’s April Fool’s Prank’, ‘Just a Couple Friends – Acoustic (Music Video)’.
