Adrian Gerald Amos Jr is a professional football player. The 29-year-old Maryland native currently plays in the National Football League and is on the roster of the Green Bay Packers. He previously had a four-year stint with the Chicago Bears who drafted him in 2015.

He has played primarily in the safety position for most of his career and is ranked amongst the top ten best players in that role.

Adrian Amos Contract

Adrian Amos signed a deal worth  $37 million, $12 million of which was guaranteed money with the rest being incentive-based bonuses. The contract also included an $11 million signing bonus. The deal will keep him with the Green Bay Packers for four years after playing with the Bears for a similar duration.

Adrian Amos Salary per year

Amos will make just over $1.1 million in base wages, with additional earnings coming from roster bonuses, restructure bonuses, and workout bonuses.
