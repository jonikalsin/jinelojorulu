LaToya J. Jackson – Professional Life

LaToya J. Jackson is a Reality Television/Radio personality. She initially gained popularity as a contestant on the 27th season of The Real World: St. Thomas. It aired in the Summer of 2012. This then earned her a spot on the 25th season of MTV’s The Challenge: Free Agents. She has since become a presenter for Da Blacktop Radio. She also worked alongside Brandon Swift on the 27th season of The Real World: St. Thomas.

After graduating from Virginia State University, she began working as a production assistant for a local news station. It was then that she was cast on The Real World. Besides the above-mentioned shows, she also appeared in Invasion of the Champions, and XXX: Dirty 30. 

She is also a business owner and she has founded the women’s accessories store The Toybox Shop. At present, she introduces herself as a Natural Skincare guru and Advice Blogger. She is also the owner of Milky Bath Co., the Raw African Black Soap Facial Sticks. It is 100% Organic, has Zero toxins, is Handmade, is Convenient, less messy, and lasts 2x longer than a soap bar.

Alongside appearing in various reality shows, she has been involved in several tasks including appearances, and speaking engagements, and is the creator/host of the online radio talk show “Toybox Radio”. It is a live online radio show that brings its listeners reality stars and celebrity interviews along with music, and celebrity gossip.
