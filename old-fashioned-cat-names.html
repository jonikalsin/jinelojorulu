Old-Fashioned Cat Names | Nameberry

May 03, 2024
blog

Old-fashioned cat names are one of the biggest trends in pet names. Vintage cat names such as Lucy and Cleo, Oliver and Leo, top the charts year after year.

Along with Lucy, other old-fashioned girl cat names include Betty, Georgia, and Susanna. In addition to Oliver, other old-fashioned boy cat names include Edwin, Percy, and Wallace. Vintage nicknames that may appeal include Effie, Lottie, Chaz, and Ozzy.

It's charming to use old-fashioned names for cats that are not back in style for humans. These include crusty-cool choices such as Enid and Ernest, Velma and Vernon.
