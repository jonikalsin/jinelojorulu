James McArdle is a Scottish stage and screen actor best known for his roles in films such as Private Quiet and Star Wars: The Force Awakens ; and television series such as Love and Marriage, Turks and Caicos , and Man in an Orange Shirt .

How old is James McArdle? Where was he educated?

James McArdle was born James John McArdle on April 3, 1989 in Glasgow, Scotland. At the time of writing this article, he is 31 years old.

McArdle grew up in Darnley, southwest of Glasgow, and attended St. Ninian’s High School in Giffnock, East Renfrewshire. McArdle started acting while he was still young and attended PACE Theater Company

He traveled to London on his own and attended the prestigious Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, though he never graduated, instead leaving to act on stage. Since then, McArdle has resided in London, England.

What is James McArdle known for?

James McArdle is a stage and screen actor. He began acting professionally on stage himself, having been a member of PACE Theater Company. He left RADA in 2010 and appeared in a Shakespearean production that same year. Macbeth in The Globe.

Since then, McArdle has been seen in plays such as Spur of the Moment at the Royal Court Theatre, Emperor and Galileo at the National Theatre, Chariots of Fire at Hampstead Theatre, and James I: The Key Shall Keep the Lock at the National Theatre . .

McArdle made his screen debut in 2011 in the made-for-television movie. Page Eight. He made his television series debut as Stephen West in the first two episodes of the ITV crime drama series. Appropriate Adult .

Since then he has had roles in television series such as Page Eight, Love and Marriage, New Worlds, Salt the Battlefield, Turks and Caicos, 37 Days , and Man in an Orange Shirt .

McArdle made his film debut in the 2012 British war drama Private Peaceful, where he appeared as Lieutenant Buckland. He is perhaps most recognized for Niv Lek’s portrayal of him in 2015 ‘s Star Wars: The Force Awakens . Besides that, he has had roles in movies like ’71, Mary Queen of Scots , and Ammonite .

He will appear as Deacon Mark Burton in the upcoming HBO limited drama series. Easttown Mare starred Kate Winslet , Julianne Nicholson , Pearce Boy , John Douglas Thompson, Sosie Bacon , and the late Phyllis Somerville , among others.
