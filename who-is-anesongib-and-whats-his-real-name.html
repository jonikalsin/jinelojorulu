Who is AnEsonGib and what's his real name?

July 13, 2024
blog

YOUTUBER and friend to The Sidemen – AnEsonGib is a well known face in the online world of FIFA players.

But what’s his real name? Here’s everything you need to know about AnEsonGib.

Who is AnEsonGib?

AnEsonGib, born July 4, 1996 is an English Saudi-Arabian YouTuber.

He started his YouTube channel in August 2012 and by November 2012 he had posted his first video – FIFA 13 gameplay.

AnEsonGib is a self-proclaimed notorious FIFA player and is well known for his Ultimate Team pack luck.

He refers to himself as the ”pack king” – due to him frequently getting high-rated players in his packs.

The YouTuber currently lives in the Stratford Halo tower with fellow YouTuber, MaxPlaysFifa.

This apartment block skyscraper in East London is associated with the Sidemen – and many of you may recognise it as The Sidemen Tower.

What is AnEsonGib’s real name?

AnEsonGib has a number of different names he is better known as in the world of YouTube – such as Big Gibber.

However, his real name is actually Ali Loui Al-Fakhri.

How did AnEsonGib become famous?

Ali made his first big breakthrough after playing YouTube stars and Sidemen members KSI and Harry Lewis – in FIFA 14 wagers.

Since then the YouTuber has done a number of FIFA collaboartions with Harry and participated in the 2016 charity football match – YouTube Allstars.

He currently has 2.31 million subscribers on his YouTube channel – with each video generating millions of views.

However, he hasn’t stopped there, in 2020 it was announced that AnEsonGib would fight Jake Paul in a professional boxing match – after previously winning two amateur boxing matches.

The match, which took place on January 2020 was held in Miami and was promoted by British sports promoter and Chairman of Matchroom Sport – Eddie Hearn.

Jake Paul won the fight in the first round – but that didn’t deter the YouTuber.

In May 2021, he took to the ring again for the second time when he fought TikToker Taylor Holder – and this time left as the winner.

He is now set to step back into the ring on July 30, 2022 to take on American star – Austin McBroom.
