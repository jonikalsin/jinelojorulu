Kai Knapp, who is 10 years old, is the only child of Alexis Knapp, who played Stacie Conrad in the Pitch Perfect movie series. She has established herself as a caring and committed mother over time, as shown by their chemistry in public and on social media.

The daughter of actor Ryan Phillippe is raised by Alexis Knapp.

In 2010, Alexis dated actor Ryan Phillippe, best known for his part in the soap opera One Life to Live, for less than a year. But after their breakup in September 2010, the actress learned she was expecting, and Kailani Merizalde Knapp was born in June 2011.

Having stated that, she did not divulge her baby’s father at first and kept mute. Fans conjectured that Ryan might be the father around this time. A source told Us Weekly that he “has known from the start that he is the father,” according to the publication.

He will not consent to a paternity test. The actor was reportedly there in the delivery room when Alexis gave birth to their daughter, according to numerous accounts. Ryan began co-parenting Kai a few months after the baby’s birth, a source told People, adding that Ryan was “well aware of the situation and fully prepared to accept responsibility if the child is his.”

In addition, the “I Know What You Did Last Summer” actor and ex-wife Reese Witherspoon have a boy and a daughter together, Deacon Elizabeth Phillippe and Ava Elizabeth Phillippe.

The chemistry between Alexis Knapp and her daughter

Alexis maintains her 300,000 Instagram followers discreetly. It appears that she routinely uploads pictures of herself and her child to the network. She took a cute selfie with her toddler, Kai, for instance, according to Dailymail, in August 2016.

In the picture, Kai was wearing a vest with an American flag pattern, and the actress smiled gorgeously for the camera. The photograph’s caption read, “Enough to make me patriotic.” Having said that, Alexis has done a great job of keeping her personal life and that of her child private. For instance, she only makes a few public appearances and has a private Instagram.

In addition, unlike many celebrities who are eager to draw attention to their children’s separate social media profiles, the actress has not set up an account for her daughter.
