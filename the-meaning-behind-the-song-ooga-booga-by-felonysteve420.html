As a music journalist, I have come across countless songs throughout my career. Some songs are catchy, some are poetic, and some leave a lasting impact on your soul. Today, I want to share with you a song that has had a profound effect on me: “OOGA BOOGA” by Felonysteve420.

I first heard this song on a lazy Sunday afternoon. I remember stumbling upon this track at a friend’s house while we were having a casual jam session. Little did I know, it would completely captivate me from the first note. The lyrics, the rhythm, and the raw emotions present in “OOGA BOOGA” spoke to me on a level that few songs have ever achieved.

Let’s dive into the song itself and unravel the meaning behind its intriguing lyrics.

In the opening verse, Felonysteve420 proudly proclaims, “Fuck you, you can’t even monetize my ads correctly.” These lines indicate a sense of frustration and defiance. It’s as if the artist is challenging the monetization and commercialization of his work. He expresses a desire for recognition and validation, ruing the inability of others to grasp his artistic vision.

Moving on to the second verse, we are greeted with a stream of consciousness. Felonysteve420 mentions seemingly unrelated images, creating a vivid collage of thoughts. The references to cereal, a harpoon, and being the “day of the doom” suggest a playful yet chaotic atmosphere. The repetition of “OOGA BOOGA” is reminiscent of a tribal chant, perhaps symbolizing a primal and untamed spirit within the artist.

Through the lines “It’s Christmas in June, Ahh, kaboom,” Felonysteve420 creates a juxtaposition of contrasting elements. This contrast could be interpreted as the artist’s way of defying societal norms and expectations. It’s a rebellion against the conventional and an embrace of the unexpected.

As the song reaches its conclusion, Felonysteve420 repeats the line “OOGA BOOGA, my tune.” Here, the artist affirms his unique identity and his connection to the song. The mention of popping a balloon and making people swoon suggests a desire to evoke powerful emotions and leave a lasting impact on listeners.

The outro brings a sense of lightness and self-awareness to the song. Felonysteve420 compares life to a “fucking cartoon,” hinting at the playfulness and absurdity of our existence. It is a reminder to not take life too seriously, but to embrace the whimsical aspects that make it beautiful and unpredictable.

With its eclectic mix of lyrics and captivating melodies, “OOGA BOOGA” embodies the essence of Felonysteve420’s artistic expression. The song encourages us to break free from societal norms, embrace our individuality, and celebrate the vibrant chaos that life has to offer.

Released on April 2, 2021, as part of Felonysteve420’s album titled “OOGA BOOGA,” this song serves as a testament to the artist’s unique talent and vision. It pushes boundaries, challenges expectations, and invites listeners on a journey of self-discovery and liberation.

In conclusion, “OOGA BOOGA” is not just a song. It is an anthem for the rebellious souls, the rule-breakers, and those in search of their own artistic voice. It reminds us that music has the power to transcend boundaries and touch our hearts in ways we never thought possible. So, let the infectious rhythms and thought-provoking lyrics of “OOGA BOOGA” guide you on a colorful and introspective musical adventure.
