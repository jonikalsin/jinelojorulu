A Comprehensive List: Top 10 Players in College Football 2024

May 03, 2024
blog

College football fans, get ready for an exhilarating season as we dive into the much-anticipated EA Sports College Football 25, set to be released on July 19, 2024. The game's top 100 player rankings have been revealed, and it's time to take a closer look at the top 10 players who are expected to make a significant impact on the virtual gridiron this year.

 

1. Will Johnson - Michigan Cornerback

Leading the pack with an impressive 96 overall rating, Will Johnson is a defensive force to be reckoned with. Coming off a stellar sophomore campaign, Johnson's standout performances, including a pivotal interception during the national championship game, have rightfully earned him the top spot. Drawing comparisons to former Alabama star cornerback Patrick Surtain II, Johnson is poised to be a game-changer in the upcoming season.

 

2. Travis Hunter - Colorado Wide Receiver and Cornerback 

Not too far behind, Colorado's versatile star, Travis Hunter, secures the sixth position with a remarkable 95 overall rating. Despite being limited to nine games in his first year, Hunter's exceptional skills on both offense and defense have earned him consensus All-American honors, making him a player to watch out for.

 

3. Will Campbell - LSU Left Tackle 

A key player on the offensive line, LSU's Will Campbell has earned the second spot in the top 10 rankings. With his exceptional technique and prowess as a left tackle, Campbell is set to be a linchpin in LSU's offensive strategies, ensuring protection for the quarterback and paving the way for successful plays.

 

4. Ollie Gordon II - Oklahoma State Halfback 

Ollie Gordon II from Oklahoma State has secured the third position with his outstanding performance as a halfback. With his agility and ability to make game-changing runs, Gordon is expected to be a driving force in Oklahoma State's offensive lineup.

 

5. Caleb Downs - Ohio State Free Safety

Ohio State's Caleb Downs has impressed with his prowess as a free safety, earning him the fifth spot in the top 10. With his defensive skills and ability to read the game, Downs is anticipated to be a significant presence in Ohio State's defensive strategies.

 

6. Malaki Starks - Georgia Free Safety 

Georgia's Malaki Starks is a standout talent in the secondary, making it to the eighth position in the top 10 rankings. With his versatility to play center field and match up in man coverage, Starks is an invaluable asset for Georgia's defense, ensuring tight coverage and strategic plays.

 

7. Mason Graham - Michigan Defensive Tackle 

Michigan's defensive tackle, Mason Graham, has made his mark with an exceptional performance, securing the fourth position. With his ability to generate pressures and disrupt offensive plays, Graham is poised to be a force to be reckoned with in Michigan's defensive lineup.

 

8. Quinshon Judkins - Ohio State Halfback 

Another standout player from Ohio State, Quinshon Judkins, has clinched the ninth position with his remarkable skills as a halfback. Judkins' ability to make impactful runs and contribute to Ohio State's offensive prowess makes him a player to keep an eye on.

 

9. Tate Ratledge - Georgia Right Guard

Georgia's Tate Ratledge has earned the tenth spot, showcasing his prowess as a right guard. With his exceptional technique and ability to create opportunities for successful offensive plays, Ratledge is anticipated to be a vital component of Georgia's offensive line.

 

10. James Pearce Jr. - Tennessee Right End 

Rounding up the top 10, Tennessee's James Pearce Jr. has secured the seventh position with his standout performance as a right end. With his defensive skills and ability to disrupt opposing offenses, Pearce is poised to be a game-changer for Tennessee's defensive strategies.

As the much-awaited EA Sports College Football 25 is set to hit the shelves, these top 10 players are expected to bring the virtual gridiron to life with their exceptional skills and game-changing performances. Get ready to witness an electrifying college football season as these players take center stage and redefine the game with their remarkable talents.
